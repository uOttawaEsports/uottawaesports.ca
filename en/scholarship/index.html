Scholarship Information

Press Release

The University of Ottawa has secured $50,000 over the next 2 years from the Ontario Esports Scholarship Program. High achieving undergraduate and graduate students at uOttawa looking to pursue a career in esports and video gaming can apply for a $2,500 annual scholarship on the uOttawa scholarships and bursaries portal.

The announcement of the scholarship fund was a result of tight collaboration between the student-run uOttawa Esports club, and passionate uOttawa faculty members. uOttawa Esports Co-President Zack Goldstein comments, “Providing esports specific scholarships to students shows how far we’ve come from a grassroots community, to an organization that is making waves in campus life. My hope is that these scholarships help uOttawa become a major scholastic esports hub not only in Ontario, but Canada as well.”

The emergence of the Ontario Esports Scholarship Program highlights the necessity to support students researching or pursuing a career in such a rapidly developing industry. The achievement of successful applicants in the realm of esports will demonstrate the need to further support on campus esports and integrate it into the student experience outside of student run programming

Students can apply for the scholarship through the scholarship and bursaries portal on uoZone. To be considered, students must:
1. be a Canadian citizen, a permanent resident, a person with the protected/refugee status or an international student;
2. enrolled as a full-time student in a Ministry-approved video-gaming, game development, game design or a similar program that may lead to a career in video-gaming and/or eSports.;
3. demonstrate financial need; and
4. demonstrate merit, which can include academic achievement and community service.

uOttawa Esports is tirelessly committed to enriching the student experience of all uOttawa gamers. Questions surrounding the new esports scholarship should be directed to Financial Aid and Awards Service. The deadline to submit an application is October 31st, 2023.
